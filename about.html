Our Story

Shree Bakes is an artisan bakery creating cakes, cookies, slices, and sweet treats in Ktown, Nepal.Originally Shree Bakes Kitchen has been part of the Ktown community for almost 10 years, selling wholesale to businesses in Ktown and across the South Asia. Now, our cakes are available to everyone.

Made with skill & passion

Cake specialists Shreesha and Shreya have more than 9 years’ professional cake-making experience between them. They’ve worked as pastry chefs and head chefs in Cafe Matina. In 2012, Shreesha started her own business supplying cakes and cookies to Raeward Fresh Ktown; her focus was on Kiwi classics handmade with real, high-quality ingredients. Shreya joined the team in 2018 and together, they’ve developed a delectable range of cakes, cookies and sweet treats for all occasions, from your wedding cake through to an indulgent morning tea treat!
